Why Sentinel

Signed Agent Identity

Agents are registered with Ed25519 keys. Every action request is signed, timestamped, and verified before processing.

Deterministic Policy

Actions flow through allow, review, or deny decisions using explicit policy rules instead of trusting model output.

Controlled Execution

Approved actions reach infrastructure only through queue-backed executor workers and safe execution adapters.
